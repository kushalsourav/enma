Avatar

Avatars allow people to represent themselves online in whatever way they want.

Example of Image Avatar

Avatars are available in 4 formats - primary , secondary , tertiary and quaternary.
To use this avatar you have to include these classes avatar followed by avatar-primary avatar-secondary avatar-tertiary avatar-quaternary and also you have to add class img-responsive img-round for avatar with images.

Example of Text Avatar

Text Avatars are available in 4 formats - primary , secondary , tertiary and quaternary.
It is same as avatars with image you just need to add this class avatar-text to position the text in center.

Badge

Badges are displayed on components and icons on your Home screen to notify you of something new in a component.

Example of Badge over avatar and icon.

We have two type of badges here badge over avatar and icon.
To use this badge over avatar you have to include these classes badge followed by status and for types status-online status-away status-away.
